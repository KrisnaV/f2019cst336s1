Worm

After got inside the system, worm becomes active and makes its way to do the specific task that it's programmed to. Often time, worm is used by the hackers to leave the back door open, so that they can steal the users' data.
Virus

Through download or open attached file in emails, virus becomes dormant. When the users open the file, then the virus is activate and start the spreading.
Trojan

By pretending to be a legitimate software, is how Trojan deliver the virus into the system. Trojan could be the files that the user download from the internet or email attachements.
