1- ILOVEYOU
This top deadly virus was created by Reonel Ramones and Onel de Guzman. The virus caused damages around the world and the worth of damage is around $10 billion. It spreads through email with TXT attached file. The infection was so great that forced the government agencies and big corpooration to temporary took their mailling system offline.
2- Code Red
The worm discovered by eEye Digital Security. It targeted computers with Microsoft IIS web server installed. After the infection, the worm makes replicate of itself and with the bug in the programming, give the replicate process even faster. It attacked some IP address and the White House website was one of them. After the attack, it leaves behind with an infected page that posted a message said "Hack by Chinese!"
3- Melissa
This virus was created by David L. Smith. It spreads through an infected Word Document posted up on alt.sex usenet group. After it's downloaded to the computer, the virus then mail itself to the top 50 people in the user's email list. It causes high volume of email usage that disrupted the email traffic of government and corporation.
4- Sasser
Dicovered in 2004. This Microsoft worm created by a computer science student named Sven Jaschan. It causes the computer to run slow and crash down. It uses the buffer overflow in LSASS to cause the crash and uses the system resources to infect other automatically through internet.
5- Zeus
Is a trojan horse targeted Windows system. The infection makes its way through drive-by download or phishing scams. It was discoved in 2009, after it caused damage to thousands of FTP accounts and computers in big corporations and banks. That includes Amazon, Oracle, Bank of America, Cisco, etc. This trojan horse was used to steal users' login credentials. Over one million computers were infected in US alone.
