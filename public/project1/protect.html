Some Tips on How to Protect Your Computer System From Malware

  • Keep your software up to date.
  • Don't click on links within email from unrecognize sender.
  • Use antivirus software (free antivirus software are also available).
  • Back up your computer regularly.
  • Use strong password.
  • Enable firewall on your system.
  • Minimize download.
  • Use a pop-up blocker.
